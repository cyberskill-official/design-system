Every operator-facing EN doc under docs/*.md must have a Vietnamese counterpart at docs/vi/<same-name>. Checks: (1) file presence — EN and VI both fetch as raw markdown (not HTML intercept); (2) no empty stubs — VI length ≥ 40% of EN (floor 80 chars) and is not a TODO/FIXME/stub placeholder; (3) stale-phrase blacklist on both languages (the retired suite/pack counts). Historical audit notes under _audit/archive/ are not tracked. Does not duplicate component/template bilingual systems (_i18n / .dc.html). Verdict on window.__docslangparity.
