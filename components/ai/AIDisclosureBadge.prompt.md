**AIDisclosureBadge** — an info-blue pill that discloses AI involvement and expands to explain what was generated and cite sources. It is an accessibility/explainability affordance, not decoration; ship EN + VN copy.

```jsx
<AIDisclosureBadge
  label="AI assisted"
  details="Draft summary generated by Lumi, reviewed by the team."
  sources={["release-notes.md", "task backlog"]}
/>
```
