# Voice & Language

This page is a working summary. The canonical source is the README's **CONTENT
FUNDAMENTALS** section — [README.md](/README.md) — with extension rules in
[docs/conventions.md](/docs/conventions.md). Where this summary and the canonical
docs disagree, the repo source wins.

## The voice is a chord

**Warm · direct · honest · respectful — all four at once.** Never "fun / playful /
edgy"; that is explicitly off-brand.

- **Warm, human, second-person.** Speak to "you"; the studio is "we"; Lumi speaks as
  "I". Gentle and encouraging, never corporate. *"Lovely. What should I call you?"*
- **Direct and concrete.** Short sentences, plain outcomes, no hedging.
  *"Send a short note. We reply within one business day."*
- **Honest about trade-offs.** The brand advertises candour — *"senior, small, and
  honest about trade-offs."* Risks are stated in words, never hidden or colour-only.
- **Respectful of consent and privacy.** Ask before contacting; promise not to share.
  *"Your details go straight to the team. We never share them."*

**The "wish" metaphor is the spine.** Products are *wishes*; Lumi (the golden genie)
*grants* them by handing them to a real human team. Verbs: *make a wish, grant, shape
it, ship, last.* Use it warmly, never gimmicky.

## Casing & mechanics

- Sentence case for headings and buttons (*"Make a wish"*, *"Tell us your wish"*).
- UPPERCASE + wide tracking only for small eyebrows, labels, table headers.
- Titles avoid trailing punctuation; body uses full stops; em dashes for asides.
- Numerals as digits for concrete SLAs (*"3 business days"*).

## Vietnamese-first

Every user-facing string ships an EN + VN pair, or carries an explicit deferral note.
Vietnamese is a first-class citizen, not a translation afterthought:

- Diacritics are preserved everywhere — *Nguyễn Hoàng Vũ*, *Hiện Thực Hoá Ý Chí*.
- Vietnamese-safe line-heights (body 1.5 / heading 1.35) leave room for stacked
  marks — see **Foundations/Typography** for the live demo.
- Components resolve their built-in strings from the nearest `lang` attribute via the
  central registry (`components/_i18n/strings.js`, EN·VI parity gate-enforced) — see
  the **I18n** section for paired stories.
- Bilingual scope boundary (documented, not a gap): emails + team/legal/finance docs
  are EN·VN; client/media collateral is EN-first.

## No emoji

Emoji are **not used** in product UI or brand copy. Meaning is carried by words and
the in-repo line-icon set; the Lumi mascot and the sparkle "wish" glyph do the
affective work.
