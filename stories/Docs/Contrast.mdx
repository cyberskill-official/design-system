# Contrast (APCA)

**Doctrine:** text sits on `-bright` or `-tint`, never on the mid-tone `-accent` — at any size. The accent is for bars, borders, progress fills, and non-text fills only.

## What we measure

- Elemental text-accent / bright / on-strong pairings (light and dark)
- Dark elemental packs: bright ≥ 75 · accent ≥ 60 · button ink ≥ 75 · ink-on-tint ≥ 75 — **15/15 packs pass**
- Semantic colours on dark surfaces carry dedicated overrides (success / danger / warning / info)
