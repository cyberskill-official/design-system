Pattern: put <image-slot> in a sized wrapper. For full-bleed over the brand ink, layer .cs-aurora-wash (opacity ~.5, pointer-events:none) and a warm umber scrim above it, content on top and click-through, so the empty drop-tile never dominates and text stays legible on any photo. Circle for avatars, fixed-height rect for cards. Slots persist via a sibling sidecar. In DC templates load the component with <x-import component-from-global-scope="image-slot" from="../../image-slot.js"> in the helmet. Best applied to: marketing-page hero, marketing-case-study, marketing-social-kit, culture-event-invite, article hero, and any team/about section.
