Highlights

✓
Opt-in glass surfaces — apply .cs-surface-* to any component after an APCA check.
✓
Verifier v2 — 0 warnings across 15 packs, with per-pack screenshot regression.
✓
Dark-mode APCA re-check — every semantic pairing re-verified at Lc ≥ 75.
