# Participant Consent Form — CyberSkill User Research

*Bilingual EN + VN. PDPL Articles 10–12 compliant per Part 8 §5. Trauma-informed per Part 5 §21.2.*

---

## English

Thank you for participating in our research session. Before we begin, we want to be clear about what we'll do with what you share with us.

### What we'll do

- Listen to your experience and ask follow-up questions.
- (If you consent below) record audio / video of the session.
- Take notes and write up what we learned.
- Use what we learned — anonymised — to improve our products.

### What we will not do

- Share your name, email, or any identifying detail outside our research team.
- Use your data for marketing or for any purpose other than this research.
- Keep your recording longer than 12 months (transcripts: 36 months).

### Your rights

- You can stop the session at any time. No reason needed.
- You can ask us to delete everything we've recorded about you. We will, within 30 days.
- You can decline to answer any question.

### Recording — please tick

- [ ] I consent to audio recording.
- [ ] I consent to video recording (face on camera).
- [ ] I consent to screen recording.
- [ ] I do **not** consent to recording. (Researcher will take notes only.)

### Quotes

- [ ] Researcher may quote me anonymously in internal reports.
- [ ] Researcher may quote me anonymously in external publications (blog posts, conference talks).
- [ ] Researcher may **not** quote me at all.

### Honorarium

You will receive {amount} {currency} as a thank-you for participating. This is not contingent on what you say, and you can stop the session and still receive the honorarium.

## File handling (researcher-only, after the session)

1. Upload recording to the insight repository (Dovetail / Condens) under `Research Sessions/{YYYY-MM-DD}/{participant-id}`.
2. Strip any personally-identifying detail from the transcript before tagging.
3. Set retention: recordings 12 months; transcripts 36 months; tagged insights indefinite.
4. Per PDPL Article 22, log the upload (who, when) in the audit trail.
5. If the participant later asks for deletion, complete within 30 days per PDPL Article 18.
