# Participant Recruiting Playbook

*Closes audit recommendation §14.10 ("research-cohort recruiting playbook"). Pillar 3 — Recruitment per Part 10 §16.4.*

---

## Diversity floor (audit B5.7 path)

Every research cohort over 5 participants must include:

- ≥ 30% participants from each major locale of the product's audience (per Part 5 §13)
- ≥ 1 participant using assistive tech (screen reader / keyboard-only / switch / voice control) per Part 5 §16
- ≥ 1 participant from a low-bandwidth context (mobile data; WCAG 2.5 considerations)
- ≥ 1 lived-experience consultant for trauma-informed surfaces (Part 5 §21.2 trauma-informed list)

Cohorts under 5 participants apply the floor to the *quarter*, not the session.

## Sources

| Source | Use case | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Existing customers (opt-in) | Highest validity; small pool | Honoraria |
| User-research panels (User Interviews, Respondent.io) | Diverse, fast | Per-recruit fee |
| Local Vietnamese university student panels | Vietnamese-locale validity | Modest honoraria; ethics review per Part 8 §5 |
| Lived-experience consultancy (per Part 5 §21.2) | Trauma-informed surfaces, accessibility | Per-engagement |

| Region | 30 min interview | 60 min usability | Diary study (1 week) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Vietnam | 500,000 ₫ | 1,000,000 ₫ | 5,000,000 ₫ |
| EU | €40 | €80 | €400 |
| US | $50 | $100 | $500 |
| Asia (other) | $30 equivalent | $60 equivalent | $300 equivalent |

Honoraria are **not** contingent on what the participant says (per `consent-form.md` § Honorarium).

## Screening

Use a 3-question screener:
1. Do you have ≥ 6 months experience with {product class}? (Filter out novices unless we want them.)
2. What devices / assistive tech do you use? (Capture for the diversity floor.)
3. Are you available within the next 14 days for a 30–45 min session? (Schedule slack.)

## Scheduling

Per Part 10 §16.3 continuous-discovery cadence: ≥ 4 interviews per active product per month. Sessions are weekly; never longer than 60 min; followed by a ≥ 15-minute synthesis block.

## Ethics review

Per Part 8 §5 PDPL governance:
- Sensitive flows (medical, legal, financial-hardship) require an additional ethics check before recruiting.
- Minors (< 18) require parent/guardian consent + age-gating per §14.6 expansion item.
- Recordings handled per `consent-form.md` retention policy.
